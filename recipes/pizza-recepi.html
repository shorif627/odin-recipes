Pizza recepi

Description

A pizza is very delicious dish orginally from Italy

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce (or pizza sauce)
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Toppings of choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.)
  • Dried oregano or Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone and brush lightly with olive oil.
  4. Spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese generously on top of the sauce.
  6. Add your chosen toppings evenly across the pizza.
  7. Season with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  8. Bake in the oven for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove from the oven, sprinkle with fresh basil if desired, slice, and serve hot.
