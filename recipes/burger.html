A burger recepi

Description

A burger is a very delicious dish

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (about 1 lb / 450 g)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Olive oil or butter (for cooking)
  • Cheese slices (optional, e.g., cheddar)
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise

Instructions

  1. Shape the ground beef into patties, about the size of your burger buns. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil or butter in a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the patties for 3–4 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired doneness.
  4. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking and cover to melt.
  5. Toast the burger buns lightly in the pan or oven.
  6. Assemble the burger: place lettuce on the bottom bun, followed by the patty, tomato, onion, and pickles.
  7. Add condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise.
  8. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.
