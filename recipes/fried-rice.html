fried rice recipes

descripton

Fried rice is a popular dish in several countries

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Eggs
  • Frozen peas
  • Cooked rice (about 3 cups, cold leftover rice works best)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the garlic (if using) and stir-fry briefly until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
  3. Push the garlic to the side and crack the eggs into the pan. Scramble them until just set, then mix them with the garlic.
  4. Add the frozen peas and stir-fry until they’re heated through.
  5. Stir in the cooked rice. Break up any clumps and toss everything together.
  6. Drizzle in the soy sauce and sesame oil, stirring well so the rice is evenly coated and seasoned.
  7. Taste and adjust with more soy sauce or sesame oil if desired.
  8. Serve hot, straight from the pan.
